Sluice Gate

Figure 121: Sluice gate geometry

The sluice gate is the upstream element of a channel and is illustrated in Figure 121. The element downstream of a sluice gate should be a straight channel element. The interesting point is that the gate height may be part of the backwater curve, but it does not have to. If the lower point of the gate is higher than the fluid surface, it will not be part of the backwater curve.
