*DAMAGE INITIATION

Keyword type: model definition, material

This option is used to define the properties of a damage initiation model. There is one required parameter CRITERION. Two criteria are available: RICE TRACEY and JOHNSON COOK, cf. Section 6.8.19.


First line:

  • *DAMAGE INITIATION
  • Enter the CRITERION parameter and its value

Following line for CRITERION=RICE TRACEY:

  • .
  • value of the damage initiation criterion at the onset of damage.

Following lines for CRITERION=JOHNSON COOK:

First line of pair:

  • (melt temperature)
  • (transition temperature )
  • (reference strain rate )

Second line of pair:

  • value of the damage initiation criterion at the onset of damage. 

Example:

*DAMAGE INITIATION,CRITERION=JOHNSON COOK
1.,1.,-1.,0.,1.,1000.,500.,1.E-2,
0.045

defines a damage initiation model according to the Johnson-Cook model with , , , , , and .


Example files: damage1, damage2.
