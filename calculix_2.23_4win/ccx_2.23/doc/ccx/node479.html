Result output

The results are stored in printoutfluidfem.f, printoutfacefem.f and frdfluidfem.f. Depending on the output requests additional information may have been calculated in calcstressheatfluxfem.f, extrapolatefem.f and calcmach.f. For what fluid increments the results are stored is controlled by the value of the parameter FREQUENCYF on the *NODE FILE etc... cards.
