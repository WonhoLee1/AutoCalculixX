CREATING QUADRATIC TETRAHEDRAL ELEMENTS.

Linear tetrahedral elements usually perform not very well, unless you have an extremely dense mesh. Therefore, it is much better to resort to 10-node quadratic elements by inserting middle nodes on each edge.
