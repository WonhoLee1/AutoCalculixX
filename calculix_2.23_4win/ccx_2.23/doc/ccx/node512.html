removesliver.f

After projection so called sliver elements may exist, i.e. elements with nearly zero volume although the area of all their faces is finite [31]. These elements are removed. A sliver satisfies the following conditions:

  • all its nodes are external AND
  • the quality exceeds the value of 10 AND
  • the element does not contain a sharp edge AND
  • and it has at least 4 external edges.
